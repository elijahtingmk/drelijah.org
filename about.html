About

Dr. Elijah Ting

Executive L&D Consultant & Certified Resilience Coach

Elijah holds an Executive Doctorate in Learning and Development from East Bridge University, Paris. As an educator and behavioural strategist, he blends organisational learning design with advanced neuroscience-based resilience frameworks to help corporate teams thrive under pressure. The title ‘Dr.’ is used strictly in this academic and professional L&D context.

My foundation began in nursing, where I learned to stay composed when stakes were high. Through years of pastoral practice, I deepened my understanding of meaning, suffering, and human connection. An Executive MBA, an Executive Doctorate in Learning & Development, and ongoing PhD research then strengthened my work in leadership, adult learning, and measurable capability development.

Today I deliver two clearly bounded solutions for Malaysian organisations. As a DOSH-certified Psychosocial Trained Person, I conduct system-level PRisMA OSH compliance screening. As a Certified Resilience Coach and HRD Corp Accredited Trainer, I deliver proactive, non-clinical workplace resilience training. Both are preventative and OSH system-level — neither is clinical psychological therapy or medical care.

How This Practice Is Positioned

A DOSH-certified Psychosocial Trained Person (PTP/ASV-053/26) and HRD Corp Accredited Trainer delivering the full PRisMA 2024 cycle for Malaysian employers — Stage I screening (LEO26), Stage II assessment (EPC23), Stage III training, and the 12-month statutory re-evaluation, under one practice.
