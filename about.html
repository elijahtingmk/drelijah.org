THE STORY

Dr Elijah Ting

Ting Moi Kieng · 陈梅钦

SIBU · SARAWAK · MALAYSIA

I started in nursing — learning to read the human body, stay calm under pressure, and care for people at their most vulnerable. That became the foundation for everything that followed.

Ministry came next. A Master of Divinity deepened my understanding of how people make meaning under suffering — and how identity, story, and community shape resilience long before any technique does. I still serve as a senior pastor across bilingual congregations in Sarawak.

The corporate world followed — an Executive MBA, then an Executive Doctorate in Learning and Development, where I built the EMPATH™ framework integrating nonviolent communication with leadership effectiveness. Alongside that work, I published The Transformation Architect™ (2026) — a quick-start guide to building signature training programmes.
