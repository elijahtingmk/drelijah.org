THE METHODOLOGY

Built on PR6 Resilience Science

I.

PR6 Model

Six evidence-based domains of human resilience.
Vision, Composure, Reasoning, Tenacity, Collaboration, and Health — each measured, profiled, and developed through a structured, science-backed approach that gives participants a clear picture of where they stand and what to build.

II.

Live Measurement

Real-time PR6 assessment via QR code.
Every participant receives a personal resilience baseline at the start of each programme — making the learning immediately relevant, data-driven, and individually meaningful rather than generic.

III.

1-Month App Reinforcement

Learning that extends beyond the training room.
App-driven reinforcement over 30 days keeps skills active, builds habits progressively, and ensures the investment in resilience development translates into sustainable behaviour change.

IV.

Onsite & Virtual Delivery

Delivered flexibly to meet your teams where they are.
From full-day bootcamps to half-day workshops and multi-week consulting engagements — programmes are designed to fit operational reality without compromising depth or impact.
