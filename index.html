How the Approach Works

Measure. Build. Sustain.

1

PR6 Model

Six domains of workplace resilience.
PR6 helps leaders and teams understand where resilience is strong, where it is vulnerable, and what needs to be built next — the methodology that informs Stage III control design.

2

PR6E Capability Assessment

Baseline, insights, and re-assessment.
PR6E supports targeted development through individual capability profiles, de-identified cohort insights, and a clearer view of progress over time. Complementary to — not a substitute for — statutory LEO26 and EPC23.

3

Reinforcement Beyond the Room

Reduce training decay.
Reinforcement support — including the Driven Resilience App where appropriate — helps sustain habits, repetition, and practical application after workshops and intensives.

4

End-to-End Cycle Continuity

One practice across the full cycle.
PRisMA Stage I, II, III, and the 12-month re-evaluation conducted within one practice — so findings translate cleanly into controls, and controls are measured against the next assessment.
