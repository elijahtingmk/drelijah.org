THE METHODOLOGY

A Clear Pathway from Assessment to Adoption

1

PR6 Model

Six domains of workplace resilience.
PR6 helps leaders and teams understand where resilience is strong, where it is vulnerable, and what needs to be built next.

2

PR6E Measurement

Baseline, insights, and re-measurement.
PR6E supports targeted development through individual assessment, de-identified cohort insights, and a clearer view of progress over time.

3

Reinforcement Beyond the Room

Reduce training decay.
Optional reinforcement through the Driven Resilience App helps sustain habits, repetition, and practical application after workshops and intensives.

4

Flexible Delivery

Built for organisational reality.
Delivery can be onsite or virtual, from short workshops to intensives and consulting pathways, without losing focus on practical transfer and measurable value.
