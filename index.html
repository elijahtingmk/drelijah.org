THE METHODOLOGY

Built on PR6 Resilience Science

1

PR6 Model

Six evidence-based domains of human resilience.
Vision, Composure, Reasoning, Tenacity, Collaboration, and Health — each measured, profiled, and developed through a structured, science-backed approach that gives participants a clear picture of where they stand and what to build.

2

PR6E Measurement

Baseline and re-measurement for measurable capability uplift.
PR6E is included in PRE and Intensives where specified, and available as a Team Pulse + HR Insights Briefing — giving teams a clear, de-identified cohort snapshot and practical priorities.

3

Driven Resilience App Reinforcement

Reinforcement beyond the room (1–3 months).
The Driven Resilience App can be included to sustain habits, increase skill repetition, and reduce training decay after workshops and intensives.

4

Onsite & Virtual Delivery

Delivered flexibly to meet your teams where they are.
From 4-hour training sessions to 1–2 day intensives and multi-week consulting engagements — programmes are designed to fit operational reality without compromising depth or impact.
